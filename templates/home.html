WHAT WE DO

Training

We work with individuals as well as with architecture practices; whichever the case, we can provide you with our best Revit training and BIM implementation services. Our approach is based on understanding your unique requirements and we will adapt our ways in order to achieve the most fulfilling experience for you.

We are deeply pationate about the process of learning and we will lead you through the difficult path of acquiring new skill.


Development

If you didn't know that by now, we love coding! We develop custom tools to provide architects with really unique ways to achieve their specific goals. We have written interactive and playful Processing snippets, we have taken our prototypes out there using Arduino (Genuino, cough), we develop Revit Add-Ins using C# and the Revit API and we have also done all the work for this website using Python and the all-mighty Django. Whichever direction your project takes you, we are ready to follow.
