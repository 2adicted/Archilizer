Who we are

Archilizer is the computationally oriented start-up of architect Deyan Nenov.
Deyan Nenov: "I have always been passionate by computer science and its application in the field of Architecture. As an early adopter of BIM (2009), I have evangelized its potential and use through trainings, lectures and above all - by personally showcasing its ability to automate staple processes of the design workflow, thus increasing precision, accuracy and efficiency and allowing us, architects, to spend more time in what matters most - designing."

We are focused on creating and implementing robust solutions by developing custom algorithms with various application in the design process. We also engage in computational in nature design projects - we have won several competitions in urban prototyping and have exhibited in V&A London.
